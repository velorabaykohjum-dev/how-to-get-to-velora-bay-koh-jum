*Important* For all arrivals: When you arrive at Koh Jum Pier, our staff will be waiting to pick you up at the pier with a tuk-tuk and a sign displaying our hotel's name. Please walk slightly outside the pier area to meet our staff. The tuk-tuk service is complimentary (free of charge).

‚úàÔ∏è From Krabi Airport

There are 2 ways for you to travel from the airport to our hotel.

  • Private transportation: We will arrange a private transfer for you from the airport to Laem Kruat Pier. Following that, you will take a private boat across to Mutu Pier. Upon your arrival, our staff will be waiting for you at the pier with a tuk-tuk to take you to our hotel. The total cost is 2,500 Baht for a maximum of 4 guests. If your group consists of more than 4 people, the cost will be 3,000 Baht.
  • Combined transportation: We will provide a private transfer from the airport to Laem Kruat Pier. Following that, you can take a public passenger ferry across to Mutu Pier. Upon your arrival, our staff will be waiting for you at the pier with a tuk-tuk to take you to our hotel.
      The costs are as follows:
    • Private Transfer: 1,000 Baht (for a maximum of 4 people) or 1,500 Baht (for more than 4 people). Payment is made at the counter upon your arrival at the hotel.
    • Public Ferry: 140 Baht per person, payable at the pier.
    • Tuk-tuk service is complimentary (free of charge).

To travel from Lanta, you can take a passenger boat directly to Koh Jum. There are both ferries and speedboats available.

  • Traveling by Ferry: board the boat at Saladan Pier and travel directly to Koh Jum Pier. The ticket price is approximately 550 Baht per person. The ferry departs from Saladan Pier at 8:15 AM and arrives at Koh Jum Pier at 9:15 AM. The estimated travel time is 60 minutes.
  • Traveling by Speedboat: board the boat at Saladan Pier and travel directly to Koh Jum Pier. The ticket price is approximately 800 Baht per person. There are two departure windows: 9:00 AM‚Äì9:30 AM and 1:30 PM‚Äì2:00 PM. The estimated travel time is about 30 minutes.
